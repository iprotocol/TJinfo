About Me


My name is Mr. Jacobsen but please call me Mr. J. This '21-'22' school year will be my sixth year teaching but only my second year with EAHS.

Before teaching at Easton, I was a freshman cross country coach, AMC math team coordinator and an Algebra 2 teacher for Don Bosco Prep in upstate New Jersey. I also started the Ultimate Frisbee club and am looking forward to starting one here when we are able to play again. In the photo to the left, you will see my Husky Max. He joins me in my other favorite past times of hiking, biking and running..

Teaching Philosophy

Math can be a difficult class; let's be honest.

However, Algebra and Pre-Calculus can both be exciting in that it allows you (for the first time ever) to make predictions about the future by using an equation that models what you are working with. It also lets you represent information in new ways that we couldn't beforehand.

I find that as a teacher it is my unique role to not only teach students how to use math, but to enjoy it as well
